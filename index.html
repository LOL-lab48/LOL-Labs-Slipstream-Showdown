What is Slipstream Showdown?

A competitive waterslide racing sport where teams build homemade sleds using cardboard, tape, and plastic bags. Riders race simultaneously—stay on your sled or you're out.

Core Rules

  • Mass start races (multiple riders at once)
  • Finish first to win
  • If you fall off → DNF (Did Not Finish)
  • Helmet is mandatory
  • No pushing, grabbing, or interference

Sled Materials

  • Allowed: Cardboard, tape, plastic bags
  • Not allowed: Metal, wood, rigid plastics, sharp materials

Season Structure

Rookie Season

  • Points-based system across multiple races
  • Final standings determined by total points
  • May include cash prizes for top teams

Main Season

  • Championship-style competition (like F1)
  • No cumulative points
  • Final placement determined at the end of the event

Teams may participate in both rookie and main events in the same season, but must use different riders for each.

Teams

  • 1–4 members per team
  • Teams recruit their own riders
  • Each team submits a sled for inspection

Teams may create identities, themes, and designs. Example team: The Soggy Rockets 🚀
